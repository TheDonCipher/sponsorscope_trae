# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive community.

## Expectations
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good faith; seek clarity when uncertain.
- Protect privacy and do not post sensitive information.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing, sharing private data, or encouraging unsafe behavior.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private issue or contact maintainers. We will review and act promptly.

## Enforcement
Violations may lead to warnings, issue/PR moderation, or removal from participation.
